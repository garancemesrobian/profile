Hello, my name is Garance and I am a first year BBA student at ESSEC. Three years ago, I embarked on the craziest experience of my life, when I left for my exchange year in the USA. This journey was all organized by the amazing team of WEP :



WEP

My host family:

I was hosted by the Neubecker family, where I got to meet one of my best friends, Alaina ! My favorite thing to do with her was going to the gym.

My high school:

I was enrolled at Ogemaw Heights High School, where I got to study Physical science, math, psychology, and even criminology! I loved doing all the group projects.

The sports:

I participated in three sports during my exchange year: Volleyball in the fall, Wrestling in the winter, and Soccer in the spring ! My favorite sport was wrestling and made the best memories during the season.

My favorite moment:

I enjoyed prom the most because I was looking forward to it. It was fun getting to dress up and dance with all my classmates.

My best exchange tips !

For any person who wants to embark on this amazing journey, my best advice would be to be open minded and always say yes to new experiences. Going out of your comfort zone can be scary but is necessary for growth.
